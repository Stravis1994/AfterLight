Welcome to Afterlight

Afterlight is a platform dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of dreams. Here, you can record your dreams, share them with others, and delve into the interpretations of various dream symbols and themes. Whether you're a seasoned dreamer or just starting to explore the realm of dreams, Afterlight offers a space for you to connect with others and gain insights into the mysteries of the subconscious mind.

Features

  • Dream Journal: Record your dreams in a private journal. You can add details, emotions, and even images to capture the essence of your dreams.
  • Community Sharing: Share your dreams with the Afterlight community. Connect with others who have similar dream experiences and gain new perspectives.
  • Dream Interpretation: Explore interpretations of common dream symbols and themes. Learn about the possible meanings behind your dreams and how they relate to your waking life.
